## Billing & Payment

### How It Works

1. Create a DocuSapiens account (free)
2. Deploy unlimited public repo docs (free)
3. When ready, upgrade to Basic/Premium
4. Monthly billing begins on upgrade date
5. Cancel anytime, no lock-in contracts

### Payment Methods

- Credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfer (for Premium/Enterprise)
- Invoicing available (certain plans)

### Billing Cycle

- **Monthly** — €10/month, charged on same day each month
- **Annual** — €100/year (save 15%), charged once per year
- **Enterprise** — Custom terms for Premium plans

### Free Trial for Basic

- 14-day free trial of Basic plan (no card required)
- Full access to all Basic features
- Automatically converts to paid after 14 days (if card added)

## FAQ: Pricing

### Q: Can I use the Free plan forever?

**A:** Yes. Free plan never expires. It's unlimited for public repositories.

### Q: Are there any hidden fees?

**A:** No. Prices listed are final. No setup fees, no overage charges (except API usage on Premium).

### Q: What if I hit my monthly limit?

**A:** Your site continues to work. AI chat requests are rate-limited, but your documentation stays live. Upgrade to raise limits.

### Q: Do I have to pay upfront?

**A:** Only when you upgrade to Basic/Premium. Free plan requires no payment or card.

### Q: Can I downgrade after upgrading?

**A:** Yes, downgrade any time to Free plan. Refunds are pro-rated.

### Q: What's included in "Support"?

**A:** Free = community (GitHub Issues). Basic = email support (24-48h response). Premium = priority phone/email (4h response).

### Q: Can I get a discount for annual billing?

**A:** Yes. Annual plans are 15% cheaper than monthly.

### Q: Do you offer educational discounts?

**A:** Not yet, but we're open to it. Contact us at support@docusapiens.ai.

### Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?

**A:** Your documentation remains on GitHub. Your deployed sites become inactive (domain goes down). Reactivate anytime by signing up again.
